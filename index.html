Team Registration

Members: Genki Aikawa, Beijun Desai, Shashank Ganeshan, Morgan Riley



Synopsis

Team Registration is currently evaluating the design of Athena. Athena is a student information system used by UGA students for various reasons such as academic and billing information, financial aid records, and searching and registering for classes. Team Registration is primarily concerned with the registration aspect of Athena. As students who have used Athena to register, we would like to improve upon the shortcomings on Athena by understanding and defining its problems, thinking about design alternatives, and creating some testable prototypes.
